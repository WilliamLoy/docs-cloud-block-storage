=========================
Pricing and service level
=========================

Cloud Block Storage is part of the Rackspace Cloud and your use of Cloud
Block Storage is billed according to the pricing schedule on the Cloud
Block Storage page at
`www.rackspace.com <http://www.rackspace.com/cloud/block-storage/>`__.

The Service Level Agreement (SLA) for Cloud Block Storage is available
on the `Rackspace Legal
Information <http://www.rackspace.com/information/legal/cloud/sla?page=blockstorage#cloud_block_storage_sla>`__
page.
